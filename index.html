Visit Lagos

Welcome to Lagos, Nigeria!
Welcome to the center of excellence of Nigeria.
Lagos offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity,
whether you're here for business or pleasure, there's something exciting around every corner of Lagos from the lively markets, cultural heritage and stunning beaches to world-class entertainment and dining.

Visit Beaches

Lagos is home to beautiful beaches like Tarkwa Bay, Elegushi Beach, and Lekki Beach. These serene coastal spots offer a mix of relaxation, water sports, and vibrant beach parties. Visitors can enjoy the sun, surf, and lively atmosphere, making Lagos a popular destination for beach lovers.

Visit National museum

The National Museum Lagos, which showcases Nigeria's vast history and heritage. The museum which was founded in 1957 by the English archaeologist Kenneth Murray, with the main purpose of preserving the different historical artifacts of Nigeria such as archaeological and ethnographic collections as well as traditional art.

Lagos festivals

They are a range of different festivals in lagos, from the Lagos Carnival in January, the Eko International Film Festival in March, the Lagos Black Heritage Carnival in April, the Lagos Photo Festival in November, the Book & Art Festival in November and the Lagos Food Festival in December.
